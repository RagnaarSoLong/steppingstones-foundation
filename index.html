Our Mission

"Stepping Stones Foundation" is dedicated to fostering an inclusive community for individuals with disabilities, including autism, blindness, and disabled veterans. We aim to break down societal barriers through advocacy and education, ensuring equal opportunities for all. By providing support, promoting awareness, and championing the rights of our members, we strive to create a world where diversity is celebrated, and everyone can thrive.
